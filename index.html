ABOUT THE SPEAKER

Daniel Young is a multi-faceted and dynamic serial entrepreneur with a diverse range of interests and a track record of success across various industries. With a passion for aviation and aerospace, education, manufacturing, food, training, construction, and numerous other sectors, Daniel’s entrepreneurial journey which began since 1985, has been marked by a constant pursuit of excellence and innovation.

With a natural inclination towards identifying opportunities and a bold determination to turn them into profitable ventures, Daniel has established himself as a seasoned businessman. His thirst for knowledge and his unwavering commitment to personal growth have been key drivers of his success, allowing him to stay ahead in an ever-evolving business landscape in Nigeria.

An avid follower of advancements in aviation and aerospace, Daniel’s deep understanding of the industry has enabled him to capitalize on emerging trends and foster ground-breaking ideas in manpower development across sectors in the Nigerian aviation space. His ventures in this sector have ranged from airline consultancy and marketing to aviation training, always with an eye for improving safety and efficiency.

Education has also been a focal point for Daniel, as he recognizes its transformative power. By investing in educational initiatives, he has helped shape future generations and equip them with essential skills.

Daniel’s entrepreneurial prowess extends beyond these fields, as he has successfully ventured into manufacturing, food production and delivery, training, construction, and more. Whether it’s establishing efficient production processes, introducing innovative dining experiences, providing high-quality training programs, or undertaking complex construction projects, Daniel has consistently demonstrated an astute business acumen and an ability to thrive in challenging environments.

Recognizing the importance of sharing his knowledge and experiences, Daniel actively participates in various industry events and initiatives. One such notable contribution is his involvement in the Retirement where he imparts valuable insights on how to start a successful business before or after retirement. Through this platform, Daniel encourages and guides aspiring entrepreneurs to realize their dreams and create their own paths to financial independence. Make plans to participate in the forthcoming Retirement Revolution Class 2024.

WHY JOIN US?

01

Supporting Career Transition

Retirement often marks a significant career transition for civil servants who are used to the stability and structure provided by their government positions. This RETIREMENT REVOLUTION TRAINING can provide essential guidance and support to civil servants who are considering starting their own business. It can help them navigate the challenges and uncertainties of entrepreneurship and make a successful transition to their new career path.

02

Utilizing Knowledge and Experience

Most retiring civil servants possess valuable knowledge and experience gained through their government roles. RETIREMENT REVOLUTION TRAINING will help them leverage these skills to start and run their own businesses effectively. By providing specialized training in areas like business planning, marketing, financial management, and legal considerations, civil servants can optimize their expertise and increase their chances of entrepreneurial success.

03

Enhanced Retirement Income

For civil servants concerned about their financial security in retirement, starting a business can provide an additional source of income. Retirement training can guide civil servants in developing profitable business models and understanding the financial aspects of entrepreneurship. By supporting the creation of successful businesses, retirement training can contribute to the financial well-being and stability of retired civil servants.

COURSE OUTLINE

This RETIREMENT REVOLUTION TRAINING Course is designed to equip individuals entering retirement with the knowledge and skills necessary to start and run a successful business. Participants will learn various aspects of business planning, budgeting, marketing, legal requirements, and more. By the end of the course, participants will be confident in starting their own businesses and utilizing their skills and experience effectively in the new venture.

COURSE DURATION: 2 Days(16 Sessions)

01. INTRODUCTION TO ENTREPRENEURSHIP

  1. Understanding the mindset and traits of successful entrepreneurs
  2. Identifying business opportunities in retirement

02. BUILDING A BUSINESS PLAN

  1. Importance of a well-structured business plan
  2. Identifying target customers and conducting market research
  3. Outlining products/services, pricing, and competitive analysis
  4. Evaluating Personal goals, skills and interest

03. IDENTIFYING LEGAL STRUCTURE AND REQUIREMENTS

  1. Different legal structures for businesses (sole proprietorship, partnership, LLC, corporation)
  2. Registering a business and obtaining necessary licenses and permits
  3. Understanding tax obligations and legal compliance

04. FINANCIAL PLANNING AND FUNDING OPTIONS

  1. Creating a startup budget and projecting financials
  2. Obtaining financing options available for retired entrepreneurs
  3. Outlining products/services, pricing, and competitive analysis
  4. Managing personal finances along with business expenses

05. MARKETING AND BRANDING

  1. Developing a marketing strategy and identifying target markets
  2. Building a strong brand presence and unique selling proposition
  3. Utilizing online and offline marketing channels effectively

06. SALES AND CUSTOMER RELATIONSHIP MANAGEMENT

  1. Understanding the sales process and sales techniques
  2. Building strong relationships and delivering exceptional customer service
  3. Utilizing customer relationship management (CRM) tools effectively

07. OPERATIONS AND LOGISTICS

  1. Managing day-to-day operations efficiently
  2. Planning for an exit strategy or succession planning
  3. Understanding mergers and acquisitions for retirement businesses

08. GROWTH STRATEGIES AND SUCCESSION PLANNING

  1. Managing day-to-day operations efficiently
  2. Planning for an exit strategy or succession planning
  3. Understanding mergers and acquisitions for retirement businesses
